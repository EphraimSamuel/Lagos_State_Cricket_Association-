IBEJU LEKKI CRICKET CLUB CROWNED CHAMPIONS !!!

Ibeju Lekki Cricket Club Crowned Champions Ibeju Lekki Cricket Club were crowned champions of the Lagos T20 Knock-Out Cricket Tournament on Sunday, after defeating Pioneers Cricket Club in the final. The match was played at the Unilag Cricket Oval in Lagos and was well-attended by spectators. Ibeju Lekki won the toss and elected to field first. Pioneers were restricted to a total of 64 runs all out, with Azeez Salau taking 3 wickets for 12 runs. In reply, Ibeju Lekki chased down the target with 6 wickets to spare, with Abiodun Bello top-scoring with 28 runs. The victory was a fitting reward for Ibeju Lekki, who had been the most consistent team throughout the tournament. They won all of their group matches and then defeated Ebute Meta Cricket Club in the semi-finals. The victory was also a major boost for the development of cricket in Ibeju Lekki. The club has been working hard to promote the game in the area and the success of the team will help to inspire more people to take up the sport. Additional details: The victory was a major achievement for Ibeju Lekki Cricket Club, who are a relatively new team. The club is hoping to build on this success and become a major force in Lagos cricket. The club is also planning to start a women's cricket team in the near future. The club is committed to promoting cricket in Ibeju Lekki and making the sport more accessible to people of all ages and abilities.
