We Are The LSCA!!!!

The Lagos Cricket Stakeholders (LCS) is a non-governmental organization (NGO) that was founded in May 2021 to support the growth of cricket in Lagos, Nigeria. The LCS is composed of a diverse group of stakeholders, including cricket administrators, coaches, umpires, players, and fans.

The organization's goals include:
Promoting the development of cricket in Lagos: The LCS works to make cricket more accessible and popular in Lagos by organizing cricket tournaments, coaching clinics, and educational programs. The organization also works to raise the profile of cricket in Lagos by lobbying the Lagos State government for support and by partnering with other organizations to promote the sport.
Increasing participation in cricket by Lagosians of all ages and genders: The LCS is committed to making cricket a sport for everyone. The organization offers programs for people of all ages and genders, and it works to break down barriers that prevent people from participating in cricket.
Providing opportunities for cricket training and education: The LCS believes that cricket education is essential for the development of the sport in Lagos. The organization offers a variety of cricket training and education programs, including coaching clinics, workshops, and online courses.
Raising the profile of cricket in Lagos: The LCS is committed to raising the profile of cricket in Lagos. The organization works to promote cricket through media coverage, social media campaigns, and public events.
