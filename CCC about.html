We Are The LSCA!!!!

The Club Cricket Committee (CCC) is a non-profit organization that was founded in 1966 to promote and develop club cricket in Lagos, Nigeria. The CCC is currently the governing body for club cricket in the state, and it is a member of the Nigeria Cricket Federation (NCF). The CCC's mission is to "promote and develop club cricket in Lagos through the provision of opportunities for all to participate and enjoy the game." The organization's goals include: To provide a forum for clubs to meet and discuss issues related to club cricket: The CCC hosts regular meetings for clubs to discuss topics such as fixtures, rules, and regulations. The organization also provides a forum for clubs to share ideas and best practices. To organize and administer club cricket tournaments and leagues: The CCC organizes a number of club cricket tournaments and leagues throughout the year. These tournaments provide opportunities for clubs to compete against each other and to improve their skills. To promote club cricket through media coverage and social media: The CCC works to promote club cricket through media coverage and social media. The organization produces a regular newsletter and it also maintains a website and social media pages. To provide training and education for club cricketers: The CCC provides training and education for club cricketers through coaching clinics, workshops, and online courses. The organization also works to develop resources for club cricketers, such as rule books and training manuals. The CCC is a valuable resource for club cricketers in Lagos. The organization's website provides information on upcoming tournaments and leagues, and it also provides resources for club cricketers. The CCC also has a social media presence where you can connect with other club cricketers and learn more about the sport. If you are interested in getting involved in club cricket in Lagos, please visit the CCC's website or contact the organization's leadership. The CCC is always looking for new players, volunteers, and supporters. Here are some of the CCC's recent activities: Organized the Lagos Cricket League, a club cricket league for teams in Lagos: The Lagos Cricket League is the premier club cricket league in Lagos. The league is open to teams of all levels, and it provides a competitive environment for clubs to improve their skills. Hosted a coaching clinic for club cricketers: The CCC hosted a coaching clinic for club cricketers. The clinic was led by experienced coaches, and it provided club cricketers with the opportunity to learn new skills and improve their game. Partnered with the NCF to organize a cricket tournament for schools in Lagos: The CCC partnered with the NCF to organize a cricket tournament for schools in Lagos. The tournament was open to all schools in the city, and it provided an opportunity for young cricketers to compete at a high level. The CCC is a growing organization with a bright future. The organization is committed to promoting and developing club cricket in Lagos, and its work is already having a positive impact on the sport in the city. The CCC is also committed to diversity and inclusion. The organization is working to make cricket more accessible to people of all backgrounds and abilities. The CCC believes that cricket is a sport for everyone, and it is working to make sure that everyone has the opportunity to participate.
